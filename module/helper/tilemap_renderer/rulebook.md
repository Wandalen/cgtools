# Lint Rulebook

Project-local lint rules for `tilemap_renderer`. These take precedence over the
workspace-wide codestyle rulebook within this crate.

---

## `#![allow]` in `lib.rs`

**Rule:** File-level `#![allow(...)]` attributes are **permitted** in `lib.rs`.

**Rationale:** The `mod_interface!` macro generates wildcard re-exports (`pub use <layer>::*`)
at its expansion site (`lib.rs`). This unavoidably triggers workspace `warn`-level lints
that cannot be suppressed at item level because the items are produced inside a proc-macro.

| Lint | Trigger |
|------|---------|
| `clippy::wildcard_imports` | `pub use <layer>::*` generated by `mod_interface!` |
| `clippy::exhaustive_structs` | re-exported public structs visible at expansion site |
| `clippy::exhaustive_enums` | same, for enums |
| `clippy::min_ident_chars` | single-char generic params in re-exported signatures |

Suppressing these at workspace level would loosen policy for every crate; suppressing
them per-item is impossible inside a proc-macro expansion. The file-level `#![allow]` in
`lib.rs` is therefore the narrowest viable scope and is explicitly permitted here.

All other lint suppressions must remain in `[workspace.lints.clippy]` in the root `Cargo.toml`.
